Our Partners

BytesToBits

BytesToBits is a coding community for everyone! You can find experienced developers to help you, or provide a solution to your problems! We also advise everyone who is new in coding and wishes to get started! We provide support for a lot of languages, as well as a website with soon-to-come lessons. We also have forums, where you can post threads with questions, or upload a project you have been working on in the resources section! We love coding, and we try to make you enjoy your journey here!

Programmer's Den

The Programmer's Den is a Discord server for people who are interested in programming! We accept people of all skill levels - so whether you are an expert or you are just starting, you will feel right at home. In The Programmer's Den, we promote a helpful community. We even have a custom bot that allows you to create a private category for your project with no permission limits. Within your category, you can add developers to it. This way, you can collaborate on anything from small hackathon projects to huge projects easily! Feel free to socialize and meet new people who have the same interests. You might even make new friends and meet new people to start awesome projects with! We even have our own forum and subreddit (r/pden). For more info, please visit our official website: https://pden.net/
